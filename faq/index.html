Frequently Asked Questions

Straight answers. Clear expectations. Professional process.

Do you guarantee a credit score increase?

No. Credit outcomes vary and depend on your unique file, creditor/bureau verification, and other factors. We provide a compliant, documented process—no promises of removals or score changes.

What exactly do you do for credit repair?

We provide administrative and educational support—helping identify potentially inaccurate, incomplete, or unverifiable items, preparing dispute packets, tracking timelines, organizing documents, and sending weekly updates.

How long does credit repair take?

Timelines vary. Many clients work in monthly dispute cycles, but outcomes depend on your file and responses from bureaus/creditors.

Do you provide legal advice?

No. We are not a law firm and do not provide legal advice. If you need legal guidance, consult a qualified attorney in your jurisdiction.

How do you handle documents securely?

We use secure sharing methods and keep organized records. We only request what is necessary and keep a clean audit trail.

Do you work with US clients even though you operate from India?

Yes. We support clients across the USA remotely with professional communication and time-zone friendly workflows.

What accounting tools do you support?

We can work with common bookkeeping workflows and tools. Tell us what you use and we'll confirm compatibility during the free consultation.

What tasks can a virtual assistant handle?

Scheduling, inbox support, admin tasks, invoicing support, CRM updates, data entry, follow-ups, and SOP-based operations.

Use the contact form. We'll ask a few questions, confirm scope, and share next steps.
